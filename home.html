About Me

On May 6th 2000, I was born in the Gambia in a small village called Bwiam where my parents resided at that time, a year later we relocated to Old Jeshwang a small town area around the Kanifing Municipal Council and it was from there i was enrolled into a nursery school called Manlut KIndergarted where i spent two years and later moved to Deeper Life Lower Basic School to start grade 1, I spent only 1 term there and was moved by my parents to Glory Baptist School where i continued from Grade 1-12. I finished and headed to The University Of The Gambia where i studied Computer Science for 4 years and graduated with my BSC. I currently work at the Standard Newspaper as ther Web Specialist and Social Media Expert, I am also an Inter Software Developer at the University Of The Gambia and I am the Chief Marketing Officer of AuuTek Gambia Limited a startup specialized in Software Development and Multimedia Creation powered by Artificial Intelligence.
